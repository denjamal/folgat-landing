Who We Are

FOLGAT works closely with national and international homeland security and defense authorities to tailor our services to the application requirements of our customers.

Advanced Materials

Innovative advanced materials and detectors, leveraging unique automated continuous crystal growth technologies to produce high-performance, uniform crystals.

These eco-friendly, high-yield processes deliver exceptional results in nuclear physics, medical imaging, and industrial applications, including custom detectors for alpha, beta, gamma, and neutron radiation. We collaborate with leading R&D centers to drive technological advancements, offering a wide array of materials and integrated detectors.
